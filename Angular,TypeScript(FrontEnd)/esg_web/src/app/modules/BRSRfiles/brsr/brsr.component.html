Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting (BRSR) is introduced by SEBI, which is developed on the basis of
National Guidelines on Responsible Business Conduct (NGRBC) principles and aligned with the UN SDGs, GRI, and United Nations Guiding Principles.

BRSR guidelines comprises of three sections –
General Disclosures
Management and Process Disclosures
Principle-wise Disclosures
Section 1

focuses on the basic set of information at the company level.

Section 2

Focuses on policy and governance level questions. Both these sections are mandatory to be reported.

Section 3

Comprises nine principles, each focused on a specialized area. Disclosures in section 3 have two categories – Essential Indicators and Leadership Indicators. The essential indicators are mandatory, while the leadership indicators are voluntary.
